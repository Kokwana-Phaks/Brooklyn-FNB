Our farming legacy

For over 30 years, Vhakanyi Farm has been a trusted name in premium livestock production in the Limpopo region.

Founded in 1992 by the Maluleke family, our farm has grown from a small homestead operation to one of Tzaneen's most respected livestock producers. We take pride in combining traditional farming wisdom with modern sustainable practices.
